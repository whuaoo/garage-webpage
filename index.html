Welcome to Garage@EEE
Official Website!


Garage@EEE is an informal Makerspace run by students for students. It is a learning platform that aims to cultivate entrepreneurial and innovative spirit. We hope to bridge the gap between classroom and experiential learning through ownership empowerment.

Events

Garage@EEE organises three main flagship events for the School of EEE, namely ENITIO, ESCENDO & IdeasJam.

The aim of these events is to foster deeper connections within the students in EEE Community, whilst exposing our student body with industrial ready skillsets.
